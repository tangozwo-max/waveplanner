🌊 Wave Planner

How it works: your sessions are stored online (Supabase) and shared with the other surfers on the grid — you always see everyone's sessions, but you can only add / edit / delete your own. Click an empty slot to add one; click one of your booked slots to change its level / side (Left / Right) or delete it.

Requesting a slot change: open one of your booked slots and set Change request → Requested to move OUT (it goes grayed out). Independently, click an empty slot, fill the wave type/side, and set Requested to book IN. Then click ✉️ Change request to generate an email to the Wave listing everything to move out and book in.
